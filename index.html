Company Profile

  Shenzhen Tengshenghui Technology Co., Ltd. was established in 2011. Manufacturing materials include copper, iron, steel, aluminum alloy, magnesium alloy, electrical wood, POM, ABS, PE, plastics, etc. Processing range includes hardware products, plastic models, molds, fixtures, automation parts, etc. Covered industries include medical devices, automobiles, drones, smartphones, sports equipment, smart robots, home appliances, industrial intelligent products, and automation equipment, etc. Capabilities include independent research and development, design, and customizing efficient specialized production fixtures and inspection tools.
